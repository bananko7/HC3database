This can usually be done through your online banking account or by visiting a branch of your bank. Here's how to do it:Log into your online banking account or visit a branch of your bank.Select the option to make a bank-to-bank transfer or external transfer.Enter the recipient's name and bank account information, including the bank name, account number, and routing number.Enter the amount of money you want to transfer and choose the account you want to transfer the money from.Review the details of the transfer and confirm the transaction.Keep in mind that bank-to-bank transfers can take several business days to complete and may have fees associated with them.
