who played dumbledore in harry potter
Dumbledore is portrayed by Richard Harris in the film adaptions of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone and Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets .After Harris' death, Michael Gambon portrayed Dumbledore for all of the remaining films.
In the Harry Potter film series, the character of Albus Dumbledore was played by actor Richard Harris in the first two films, \"Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone\" (released as \"Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone\" in the United States) and \"Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets.\" After Harris' death in 2002, the role was taken over by actor Michael Gambon, who played Dumbledore in the remaining six films of the series: \"Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban,\" \"Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire,\" \"Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix,\" \"Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince,\" \"Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1,\" and \"Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.\"","In the Harry Potter film series, the character of Albus Dumbledore was played by actor Sir Michael Gambon.
